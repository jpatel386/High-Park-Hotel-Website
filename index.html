About Us

Plan your trip today

High park hotel, located on the stunning East coast of Sri Lanka, is the place to book if you’re looking for a modern and relaxing escape. With a multi-cuisine restaurant and diving centre on site and panoramic views from every room, you don’t have to travel far to enjoy the natural beauty of Nilaveli. We’re also a great base to explore nearby attractions that the area has to offer, such as the hot water wells and pigeon islands. Book a room now in one of our chalets or cabanas for an unforgettable experience amongst the untouched paradise that is Sri Lanka.
